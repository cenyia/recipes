Ingredients:
-2 slices of American Cheese
-1 tbs of butter
-4 slices of bread


Directions:
1. In a small skillet pan, melt half the butter.
2. Combine the slices of cheese with the bread (should make 2 sandwiches)
3. Put both on the skillet so that one side of each soaks up the butter.
4. Repeat with the remaining butter on the other side of the sandwiches.
5. Keep flipping sandwiches on the skillet till both sides are brown and cheese is melted.
